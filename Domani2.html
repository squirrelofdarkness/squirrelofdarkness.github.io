On Wednesday

Classes

On Wednesday, I only had classes in the art department. I learned how to use a panel saw and a compound miter saw and did brainstorming excercises.

The Drive

After all our classes were over, my partner and I drove all the way to my house in Niceville to retrieve their backpack. I did not like how other people on I-10 were driving, but we got to and from my house safely. It was nice to see my mom again, especially since that happened to be her birthday.

The Incident

After dropping my partner back off at her dorm, I parked in back of mine. However, I accidentally pulled up too far and felt myself bump the wheelstop. I put my car in reverse to correct my parking but when I tapped on the gas, she didn't move. I had to call my insurance company to get a tow truck to pull my car off a piece of rebar that was sticking out of the wheelstop and had hooked itself on her undercarriage. It was a very long night.
